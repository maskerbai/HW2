Jintan’s story

I come from China. My hometown is a little city in Middle area in China. I have a happy family, my mother, father and little sister. My hometown to China is like Iowa to the United States. After I graduated from Senior High School in my hometown, I started to study overseas. At that time, it is totally my family’s will to let me study aboard. I have no clue what I am going to meet and what would happen to me in the future. However, after four meaningful years of college life in United States, I feel lucky I made that wise choice four years ago.

When I attended to the first lecture in the University of Iowa, I can barely understand anything that professor said. It was totally nightmare for me. I thought it was an awful decision to study aboard. However, I did not stop and give up. I watched Friends during after-class period and paid full attention on the lecture. At the end of first semester, I could understand eighty percent of the lecture.

Another big problem for me is to understand the American culture. In China, we don’t have the social form called “party” like American do. I prefer to hang out with my close friend and talked about recent lives. This kind of lifestyle is more attractive to me.

Anyway, it is fine to study overseas. Though I have lots of things to complain, the some restaurants in Iowa City are pretty nice. I enjoy the global good cultures here. I listed a couple of good Asian restaurants here. Hope someone interested in food can have a taste some time.


Jintan’s Favorite Dining Place in Iowa City

Sushi Buffet

Recommendation 4.5 stars

Satisfy your stomach with cheap price. There are lots of seafood specials on Sunday. Some Chinese traditional dishes are good for me. However, the sushi is so-so, and the dining environment is also not good.

Mekong

Recommendation 4 stars

A Vietnam Restaurant with Median price with decent dining environment; a good place for a date or a couple. Beef noodles are highly recommend there. However, the dishes are too American style.

Kapok Restaurant

Recommendation 4 stars

Taste No.1 Chinese Restaurant with decent dining environment. It cooks the real Chinese food. Some food is a little bit spicy for Americans. However, the location is too far away.
